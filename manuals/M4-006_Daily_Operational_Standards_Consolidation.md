# M4-006 --- Daily Operational Standards Consolidation

## Operating Hours

-   Operate according to the approved business schedule.
-   Employees report before opening as defined by management.
-   Opening and closing procedures are mandatory every operating day.

## Opening Standards

-   Inspect all FOH and BOH areas.
-   Verify cleanliness, equipment, utilities and supplies.
-   Verify POS equipment and opening cash.
-   Prepare dining room before customers enter.
-   Conduct pre-shift briefing.

## Closing Standards

-   Restore all operational areas.
-   Complete cleaning tasks.
-   Store products correctly.
-   Shut down equipment according to procedure.
-   Secure cash.
-   Activate security systems where available.
-   Leave the restaurant ready for the next business day.

## Food Safety

-   Maintain hygiene throughout operations.
-   Store products under required conditions.
-   Protect food from contamination.
-   Monitor product quality during receiving, storage and preparation.

## Documentation

The following records are maintained as part of daily operations: -
Opening checklist - Closing checklist - Kitchen checklist - Cash opening
and closing records - Requisitions - Purchase orders - Receiving
records - Inventory records - Cleaning logs

## Responsible Roles

-   Restaurant Manager
-   Customer Service Manager
-   Kitchen Manager
-   Cashier
-   Warehouse/Supply Manager
-   Area Supervisors
-   Assigned operational staff

## General Operational Rule

All procedures, forms, checklists and records described in this manual
shall be completed according to the documented process and retained as
operational records.
