# M3-005 --- Equipment, Inventory and Store Readiness

## Site Evaluation Form

### Evaluation Criteria

1.  Location
2.  Covered square meters
3.  Independent entrance
4.  Ability to install signage
5.  Number of rooms
6.  General building condition
7.  Floor condition
8.  Restroom condition
9.  Telephone lines
10. Lighting
11. Required permits
12. Parking availability
13. Public transportation access
14. Estimated opening timeline
15. Owned or leased premises
16. Estimated investment

### Scoring

-   Each criterion is rated from 1 to 10.
-   Final score = Sum of weighted scores ÷ 36.
