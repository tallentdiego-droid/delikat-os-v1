# M1-003 --- Operational Policies

## Policy --- Manual Ownership

-   The Confidential Operations Manual is the exclusive property of
    DELIKAT.
-   It must be returned upon expiration or termination of the franchise
    agreement.

## Policy --- Authorized Use

-   The manual may not be revealed, duplicated or used without
    authorization.
-   Breach is subject to sanctions established in the Franchise
    Agreement.

## Policy --- Manual Storage

-   When not in use, the manual shall remain locked within the
    franchise.
-   Only authorized personnel may access it.

## Policy --- Mandatory Compliance

-   Compliance with the Manual is mandatory for the Franchisee and
    personnel operating under the Franchisee's supervision.

## Policy --- Operational Updates

-   DELIKAT may revise procedures whenever required.
-   Franchisees must incorporate official revisions into the manual.

## Policy --- Verification Visits

-   DELIKAT representatives may conduct scheduled visits to verify
    implementation.
-   Visits may include control, training and advisory activities.

## Policy --- Communication

-   Official communications between Franchisee and DELIKAT shall follow
    the notification processes defined in this manual.

## Policy --- Suggestions

-   Suggestions shall be submitted using the official Suggestion Form.
-   Suggestions must follow the Franchise Agreement provisions.

## Policy --- Local Operations

-   Each franchise may establish local operating rules only if they
    remain consistent with this manual and applicable laws.

## Policy --- Franchisee Responsibility

-   The Franchisee is responsible for operation of the business and
    personnel.

## Recommendation Contained in Manual

-   Franchisees are recommended to retain qualified legal and accounting
    advisors.
