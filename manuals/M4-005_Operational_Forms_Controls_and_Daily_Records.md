# M4-005 --- Operational Forms, Controls and Daily Records

## Opening Checklists

-   Complete the opening checklist before beginning operations.
-   Record the responsible employee.
-   Supervisor verifies completion.

## Closing Checklists

-   Complete the closing checklist at the end of each operating day.
-   Responsible employee signs the checklist.
-   Customer Service Manager or designated supervisor signs the
    verification.

## Cash Controls

-   Verify opening float before service.
-   Record all cash movements.
-   Perform end-of-day reconciliation.
-   Maintain supporting documentation.

## Inventory Records

-   Maintain inventory records for kitchen and storage.
-   Record requisitions.
-   Record product receipts.
-   Record purchase orders.
-   Record inventory adjustments when authorized.

## Cleaning Records

Maintain documented cleaning records for: - General facilities. -
Bathrooms. - Kitchen. - Bar. - Service area. - Washing area. -
Warehouse. - Prep area.

## Operational Responsibilities

### Customer Service Manager

-   Verify opening.
-   Verify closing.
-   Supervise daily operations.
-   Validate operational checklists.

### Kitchen Manager

-   Supervise kitchen opening.
-   Supervise kitchen closing.
-   Schedule equipment cleaning.
-   Verify food preparation readiness.

### Cashier

-   Perform opening cash procedures.
-   Receive payments.
-   Complete cash closing.
-   Deliver required records.

## General Rule

All operational forms shall be completed truthfully, maintained as
operational records, and reviewed by the designated responsible
personnel.
