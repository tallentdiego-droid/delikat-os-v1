# M5-005 --- Vacations, Leave, Attendance and Confidentiality

## Vacations

-   Employees request vacations using the approved vacation request
    form.
-   Vacation approval is documented through the official notification.
-   Employees acknowledge receipt of vacation documentation.
-   Vacation records are retained in the personnel file.

## Leave Requests

-   Leave requests are submitted using the approved form.
-   The request records the reason, dates and authorization.

## Employee Assistance

-   Employee assistance requests are documented using the approved
    company form.

## Personal Loans

-   Personal loan requests are processed using the approved loan request
    form.
-   Loan documentation is retained in the personnel record.

## Attendance

Attendance is documented using: - Personnel time control sheet. - Manual
attendance register. - Attendance observations when applicable. -
Absence records.

## Unjustified Absence

-   Unjustified absences are documented using the official notification
    form.
-   Supporting comments are recorded when required.

## Uniform Control

-   Uniform delivery is documented.
-   Employees acknowledge receipt of uniforms.
-   Uniform policy acceptance is recorded.

## Confidentiality Agreement

-   Employees sign the confidentiality agreement.
-   Confidential information obtained during employment shall not be
    disclosed during or after employment unless authorized in writing by
    the franchisor.
-   Signed confidentiality agreements are retained in the personnel
    file.
