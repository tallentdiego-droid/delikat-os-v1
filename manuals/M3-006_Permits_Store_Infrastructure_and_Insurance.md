# M3-006 --- Permits, Store Infrastructure and Insurance

## Store Authorization

The Franchisee shall obtain all permits required by the municipality and
applicable governmental authorities.

### Typical Requirements

-   Construction permit (when applicable).
-   Commercial registration and company incorporation.
-   Operating license.
-   Architectural, electrical and sanitary plans.
-   Municipal fee payment.
-   Health authorization.
-   Alcohol sales permit (when applicable).
-   Additional permits for signage, background music or other local
    requirements.

Requirements may vary by jurisdiction. The Franchisee is responsible for
complying with local regulations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Store Infrastructure Standards

### Exterior

-   Entrance door with DELIKAT logo shall remain clean and in good
    condition.

### Lighting

-   Lighting systems shall operate safely and correctly.

### Refrigeration and Storage

-   Refrigeration equipment shall maintain legally required
    temperatures.
-   Food shall be protected from contamination.
-   Food containers shall be food-grade, covered and identified.
-   Food and non-food products shall not be stored together.
-   Food shall never be stored directly on the floor.
-   Thermometers shall be calibrated and temperatures monitored.

### Cash Register

-   Located strategically for customer flow and security.

### Cleaning Equipment

-   Buckets
-   Brooms
-   Floor cloths
-   Degreasers
-   Floor cleaners
-   Wood polish
-   Disinfecting wipes
-   Air freshener
-   Glass cleaner
-   Hand sanitizer

### Pest Control

-   Annual contract with a specialized pest control company.
-   Contract copy displayed at store entrance.
-   Waste stored in covered containers.
-   Infestations addressed immediately.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Insurance Requirements

The Franchisee shall obtain and maintain:

-   Commercial insurance.
-   General liability insurance.
-   Fire and property insurance.
-   Workers' accident insurance.
-   Business interruption insurance.
-   Food handling insurance.
-   Automobile liability insurance.

A certificate of insurance shall be submitted to the Franchisor for
approval.

Policies shall not be cancelled or modified without at least thirty (30)
days prior written notice to the Franchisor.
