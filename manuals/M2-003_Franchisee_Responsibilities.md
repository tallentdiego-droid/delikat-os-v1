# M2-003 --- Franchisee Responsibilities

## Responsibilities Toward Customers

1.  Maintain DELIKAT quality standards in service, preparation and
    presentation.
2.  Provide courteous and professional customer service.
3.  Respond promptly to customer questions and complaints.
4.  Keep the business open during agreed operating hours and according
    to local law.
5.  Provide clear, accurate and truthful product information.
6.  Strive to exceed customer expectations.
7.  Report customer satisfaction survey results.
8.  Resolve customer complaints fairly with the objective that no
    customer leaves disappointed.
9.  Ensure advertising complies with applicable laws and is not
    misleading.
10. Protect customer personal data and confidentiality.
11. Comply with all consumer protection laws.

## Responsibilities Toward Other Franchisees

1.  Operate consistently with the franchise network.
2.  Share ideas, suggestions and attend regular meetings.

## Responsibilities Toward Employees

1.  Provide a safe and pleasant workplace.
2.  Pay fair salaries.
3.  Distribute tips fairly when applicable.
4.  Provide required techniques and training.
5.  Act honestly, kindly and impartially.
6.  Promote personal and professional growth.
7.  Maintain respectful communication.
8.  Evaluate employee performance periodically.
9.  Train and supervise personnel.
10. Comply with labor laws.
11. Comply with DELIKAT work standards when compatible with local
    regulations.

## Responsibilities Toward the Franchisor

1.  Pay royalties.
2.  Maintain quality standards.
3.  Follow franchise agreement policies.
4.  Meet contractual deadlines.
5.  Respect territorial boundaries.
6.  Obtain authorization before opening additional DELIKAT units.
7.  Comply with all franchise agreements.
8.  Maintain confidentiality.
9.  Use brand according to corporate policies.
10. Submit required business reports.
11. Participate in franchisor training.
12. Receive franchisor supervision visits.

## Responsibilities Toward Government

1.  Obtain required operating permits.
2.  File mandatory declarations on time.
3.  Pay taxes, contributions and required withholdings.
