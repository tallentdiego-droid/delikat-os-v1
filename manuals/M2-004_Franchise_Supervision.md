# M2-004 --- Franchise Supervision

## Representative Visits

### Frequency

-   Representatives visit the franchise periodically.

### Franchisee Preparation

-   Prepare questions and suggestions before the visit.
-   Prepare monthly reports for review.

## Supervision Process

### Evaluation Scope

-   Customer service.
-   Knowledge of services.
-   Business operation.
-   Purchase invoices.
-   Supplier invoices.
-   Service invoices.
-   Operational documentation.
-   Cleanliness of all areas.
-   Employee interviews.
-   Employee commitment to DELIKAT.

### Communication of Results

Results may be communicated: - Telephone. - Email. - Meeting at
headquarters.

## Mystery Shopper Program

### Purpose

-   Evaluate actual customer experience.
-   Observe employee behavior anonymously.
-   Improve customer service.
-   Detect operational improvement opportunities.

### Process

1.  Anonymous visit.
2.  Completion of Mystery Shopper form.
3.  Results shared during scheduled representative visit.

## Visit Closure

After each visit: 1. Representative completes visit report. 2.
Outstanding issues are documented. 3. Operational performance is
evaluated. 4. Franchisee signs report. 5. Franchisee may add comments.
6. Franchisor retains original. 7. Franchisee receives copy.

## Purpose of Visits

-   Strengthen franchisor-franchisee relationship.
-   Improve franchise operations.
