# M3-007 --- Initial Training and Grand Opening

## Initial Training Objectives

The initial training program is designed to provide the Franchisee and
employees with the knowledge and skills required to operate the business
while maintaining DELIKAT standards.

The Franchisee shall understand in detail: - Business operations. -
Customer service procedures. - Customer loyalty objectives. - Restaurant
image standards.

## Franchisee Participation

-   The Franchisee and/or Store Manager shall attend all training
    sessions provided to staff.
-   Order and cleanliness are mandatory operational characteristics.
-   Training includes product presentation and display standards.

## Initial Training Topics

-   Brand history and concept.
-   Mission, vision and values.
-   Daily restaurant operations.
-   Food preparation.
-   Product and service knowledge.
-   Customer service.
-   Sales.
-   Inventory management.
-   Cash handling.
-   Food hygiene and safety.
-   Quality standards.
-   POS and technology.
-   Personnel management.
-   Communication.
-   Task delegation.
-   Motivation.
-   Conflict resolution.
-   Time management.
-   Marketing and promotion.

## Training Methodology

Training combines: - Theory. - Practical exercises. - Demonstrations. -
Operational follow-up.

The Franchisor also provides ongoing support and periodic training
covering new products and operational improvements.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Grand Opening

The Franchisor's marketing team coordinates with the Franchisee's local
agency to organize the opening event.

Suggested activities include: - Newspaper articles. - Newspaper
announcement congratulating the Franchisee. - Social media
publications. - Cocktail event for employees, DELIKAT management,
family, suppliers and invited guests, preferably on a different day than
the official opening.
