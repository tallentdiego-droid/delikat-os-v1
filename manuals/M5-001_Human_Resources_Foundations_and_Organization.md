# M5-001 --- Human Resources Foundations, Organization and Positions

## Purpose

Defines the organizational structure, personnel administration framework
and human resources documentation used by DELIKAT franchises.

## Manual Scope

The manual includes: - Organizational structure - Job descriptions -
Recruitment - Personnel selection - Interview process - Employment
applications - Personnel records - Performance evaluations - Competency
evaluations - Vacation administration - Leave requests - Voluntary
resignation - Employment termination - Employee assistance - Personal
loans - Attendance control - Shift control - Disciplinary actions -
Uniform control - Personal hygiene - Confidentiality agreement

## Organizational Structure

The franchise shall operate according to the approved DELIKAT
organizational chart.

## Job Descriptions

Each position shall have a documented job description defining: -
Responsibilities - Authority - Reporting relationship - Operational
duties - Required competencies

## Personnel Administration Records

The following records form part of the personnel system: - Internal
personnel requisition - Recruitment notice - Interview form - Employment
application - Complete employment application - Personnel file -
Employee evaluation - Performance evaluation - Competency evaluation -
Address declaration - Internal regulations acknowledgement - Vacation
request - Vacation notification - Vacation receipt - Leave request -
Voluntary resignation - Settlement of benefits - Employee assistance -
Personal loan request - Absence notification - Shift control - Verbal
warning - Written disciplinary notice - Suspension notice - Dismissal
notification - General dismissal form - Uniform delivery record -
Attendance control - Manual attendance register - Confidentiality
agreement

## Personnel Standards

-   Personnel records shall be maintained for each employee.
-   HR documentation shall be completed using the approved forms.
-   Employment administration follows the documented procedures
    contained in this manual.
