# M2-002 --- Franchisor Assistance

## Objective

-   Achieve the success of all DELIKAT franchises through continuous
    support.

## Assistance Provided

### Site Selection

-   Advise the franchisee on selecting the location.
-   Consider population density.
-   Consider commercial activity.
-   Consider local characteristics.

### Architectural Design

-   Deliver the Manual Arquitectónico prepared by 2C Estudio.
-   Manual defines franchise models.
-   Includes technical specifications.
-   Includes architectural specifications.
-   Includes decorative specifications.
-   Includes furniture and equipment standards.

### Launch Support

-   Supervision until store opening.

### Initial Training

Includes: - Personnel selection assistance. - Training for key
positions. - Training in DELIKAT procedures and techniques. - Initial
training at headquarters: Blvd. del Hipódromo, Colonia San Benito
No. 582, San Salvador, El Salvador. - On-site operational assistance
during initial weeks. - Trainees should previously know: - Menu and
ingredients. - Theoretical preparation processes.

### Marketing

-   Franchisor advises advertising material.
-   Franchisee pays production costs.
-   Brand Manual provided.
-   Brand Manual prepared by Etérea Collective.

### Ongoing Support

-   Continuous operational assistance.
-   Representative dedicated to franchise support.
-   Objective:
    -   Maintain quality standards.
    -   Improve revenue.
    -   Improve profitability.
    -   Supervise operational standards.

### Communications

Communication tools include: - Letters - Email - Internet - WhatsApp -
Video calls - Periodic meetings

### Operations Manual

-   Each franchise receives the Confidential Operations Manual.
