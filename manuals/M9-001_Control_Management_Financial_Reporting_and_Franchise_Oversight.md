# M9-001_Control_Management_Financial_Reporting_and_Franchise_Oversight

## Required Monthly Reports

-   State of Results and Balance Sheet.
-   Gross Sales Report.
-   Advertising Report.
-   Customer Satisfaction Survey Results.
-   Customer Count Report.
-   Submit from the 1st to the 5th of the following month or when
    requested.

## Required Annual Reports

-   Audited annual financial statements within 60 days after fiscal year
    end.

## Advertising Report Record

Capture: - Month and year. - Franchisee. - Name. - Address. -
Telephone. - Activity description. - Advertising period. - Invoice
amount. - Invoice date. - Credit note information when applicable.

## Balance Sheet Structure

Assets: - Cash and equivalents. - Investments. - Accounts receivable. -
Inventory. - Fixed assets. - Intangible assets. - Other assets.

Liabilities: - Trade debt. - Bank debt. - Labor debt. - Tax debt. -
Other debt.

Equity: - Capital. - Results. - Reserves.

## Income Statement

Track: - Transactions. - Ticket sequence. - Gross sales. - Net
revenue. - Variable costs. - Franchise fee. - Advertising fee. -
Commercial expenses. - Personnel expenses. - Taxes. - Operating
expenses. - Administrative expenses. - Operating profit.
