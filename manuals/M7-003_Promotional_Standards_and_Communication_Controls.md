# M7-003 --- Promotional Standards and Communication Controls

## Promotional Campaigns

-   Plan campaigns in advance using a semiannual or annual marketing
    calendar.
-   Coordinate product launches, seasonal products, desserts, beverages
    and special promotions.
-   Align campaign dates with relevant events and seasons.

## Point-of-Sale Promotion

-   Promotional signage shall clearly communicate new products and
    promotions.
-   Avoid excessive information that reduces message effectiveness.
-   Promotional materials shall follow DELIKAT brand standards.

## Digital Communication

-   Define the target audience before creating advertising material.
-   Prepare clear and concise messages.
-   Evaluate campaign attractiveness and results.
-   Review sales impact after campaigns.

## Customer Experience

-   Maintain an exceptional restaurant experience.
-   Encourage positive word-of-mouth through product quality and
    service.
-   Reinforce customer loyalty through consistent brand experience.

## Advertising Approval

Local advertising requests shall include: - Proposed advertising
material. - Description of the activity. - Media to be used. -
Duration. - Estimated costs. - Supporting materials. - Franchisor
approval before publication.

## Reference Assets

The manual includes example website pages, Facebook posts, Instagram
posts and advertising materials. These are treated as reference assets
and are not extracted as operational knowledge.
