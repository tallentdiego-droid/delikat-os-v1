# M3-004 --- Franchise Opening Timeline

## Weekly Execution Schedule

### General Rules

-   Week S1 begins after the Franchisor approves the proposed location.
-   Week S12 corresponds to the Grand Opening.
-   Activities indicate when each phase must begin, not their duration.

## Phases

1.  Location approval.
2.  Accounting and legal procedures.
3.  Franchise Agreement signature.
4.  Lease Agreement signature.
5.  Submit premises plans for architectural adaptation.
6.  Receive architectural project and specifications manual.
7.  Define expected opening date.
8.  Assign Store Manager.
9.  Request telephone lines and utilities.
10. Begin remodeling.
11. Recruit and select operating personnel.
12. Obtain required insurance.
13. Begin kitchen and pre-production training.
14. Begin front-of-house training and collect uniform sizes.
15. Order furniture, equipment and computer systems.
16. Order employee uniforms.
17. Deliver legal and accounting documentation.
18. Print operational forms and stationery.
19. Install furniture, equipment, signage and pole sign.
20. Select local advertising agency.
21. Present pre-opening, opening and follow-up marketing campaign.
22. Complete DELIKAT pre-opening checklist.
23. Install computer equipment and software.
24. Perform general cleaning.
25. Conduct closed-door operational tests.
26. Receive approval for Soft Opening.
27. Grand Opening.

## Daily Milestone Schedule

The manual establishes maximum target days after identifying a potential
location for each phase, culminating with:

-   Soft Opening approval: Day 77.
-   Grand Opening: Day 84.
