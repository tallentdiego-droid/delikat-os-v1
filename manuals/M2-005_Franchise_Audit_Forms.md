## Supervisor Prohibitions

-   Do not disclose collected information.
-   Do not falsify information.
-   Do not appropriate supervision materials.
-   Do not perform unrelated activities.
-   Do not collect unauthorized information.
-   Do not offer gifts or payments.
-   Do not bring unauthorized companions.
-   Do not disclose confidential customer information.
-   Do not accept gifts or benefits.
-   Do not consume alcohol during supervision.
-   Do not defame the franchisee.
-   Do not interfere with daily operations.
-   Do not modify procedures without authorization.
-   Always comply with applicable laws.
-   Violations are sanctioned under the contract.
