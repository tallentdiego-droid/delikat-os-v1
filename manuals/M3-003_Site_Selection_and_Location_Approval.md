# M3-003 --- Site Selection and Location Approval

## Site Selection

### Responsibility

-   The Franchisee is responsible for identifying potential locations.
-   Every proposed location requires prior approval from the Franchisor
    before execution.

## Franchisor Approval

The Franchisor reserves the right to approve or reject any proposed
location.

No installation work may begin until written approval has been obtained.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Store Installation

After location approval:

1.  Begin installation works.
2.  Installation project is supervised by the Franchisor.
3.  Supervision costs are borne by the Franchisee.
4.  All construction, decoration and installation costs are the
    responsibility of the Franchisee.
5.  The Franchisee is responsible for delivering the store fully
    operational before opening.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Architectural Standards

Every DELIKAT location shall comply with the criteria established in the
Manual Confidencial de Comunicación Visual prepared by 2C Estudio.

The manual defines: - Franchise models - Store dimensions - Areas -
Layouts - Equipment - Furniture - Architectural standards

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Location Evaluation Criteria

Preferred characteristics include:

-   Commercial area.
-   Medium-high to high socioeconomic zone.
-   Street location, shopping center, kiosk or similar with strong
    customer potential.
-   Structurally safe building.
-   Preference for newer buildings.
-   Rustic architectural style preferred.
-   Large patio.
-   High ceilings.
-   Large windows.
-   Modern construction materials.

Final evaluation depends on franchise model and overall suitability.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Project Timeline

Execution schedules contained in this manual are indicative only.

Final completion depends on: - Public administration timing. -
Construction progress. - Decoration progress. - Franchisee execution
speed.

The Franchise Agreement establishes the final opening deadline.
