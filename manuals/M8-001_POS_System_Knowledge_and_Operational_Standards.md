# M8-001 --- POS System Knowledge and Operational Standards

## POS Platform

-   DELIKAT standardizes operations using QUANTO POS.
-   The platform supports restaurant operations through sales,
    inventory, customer and reporting functions.

## Operational Objectives

-   Improve communication between front of house and administration.
-   Monitor restaurant performance in real time.
-   Centralize operational information.

## Core Capabilities

-   Sales management.
-   Inventory management.
-   Customer management.
-   Real-time reporting.
-   Employee permission management.
-   Warehouse management.
-   Product management.

## Sales Operations

-   Operate from mobile phone, tablet or computer.
-   Support dine-in, takeout and delivery orders.
-   Print tickets and payment receipts.
-   Track order status until dispatch.
-   Support kitchen communication through Quanto KDS.

## Inventory

-   Monitor inventory in real time.
-   Organize inventory through warehouses.
-   Maintain products and stock quantities.

## Employee Administration

-   Assign permissions according to employee role.
-   Monitor employee activity through the system.

## Reporting

-   Monitor sales in real time.
-   Review best-selling products and categories.
-   Analyze business trends.

## Training

-   QUANTO training may be provided in person or online.
-   Reinforcement videos are available through official QUANTO training
    resources.
-   DELIKAT provides instructional material for system usage.

## Licensing

-   QUANTO offers Standard and Pro plans.
-   Free trial registration is available before subscription.
