# M6-010 --- Product Manual Consolidated Operational Rules

## Standardization

-   All menu items shall follow their documented standardized recipe.
-   Ingredient quantities, yields and preparation sequence are defined
    by the recipe.
-   Recipes are the operational reference for production.

## PREP Production

-   Complete mise en place before production.
-   Produce recipe components in advance when required.
-   Cool, portion, package and label products according to the
    documented procedure.
-   Deliver finished products to warehouse after packaging.

## Kitchen Production

-   Retrieve standardized PREP components when required.
-   Complete final cooking and assembly according to the recipe.
-   Verify plating before dispatch.

## Quality Controls

-   Follow documented cooking times and temperatures.
-   Verify seasoning when indicated.
-   Compare final presentation against the approved reference before
    serving.

## Product Records

Each standardized recipe defines: - Ingredients - Quantities - Units -
Yield - Preparation - Portioning - Storage requirements
