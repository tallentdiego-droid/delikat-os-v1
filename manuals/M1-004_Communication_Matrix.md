## Required Franchisee Communications

-   Financial reports:
    -   Estado de Situación Patrimonial
    -   Estado de Resultados
    -   Balance
-   Notification of hiring and termination of key personnel.
-   Customer comments that may affect DELIKAT's image.
-   Requests for local or seasonal advertising approval.
-   Suggestions for modifications.
-   Requests for approval of franchise installations.
-   Notification of compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.
-   Notification of lawsuits, customer claims or events justifying legal
    action.

## Required Franchisor Communications

-   New operational actions.
-   Pricing policy changes.
-   Merchandising changes.
-   Procedure modifications.
-   Process changes.
-   Product or service changes.
-   New products or services.
-   Uniform changes.
-   Marketing and advertising strategies.
-   Institutional activity reports.
-   Image and decoration modifications.

## Manual Update Workflow

1.  DELIKAT issues modification notice.
2.  Franchisee replaces or inserts pages.
3.  Franchisee signs acknowledgement.
4.  Replaced pages are returned when applicable.
5.  Updated manual becomes current version.
