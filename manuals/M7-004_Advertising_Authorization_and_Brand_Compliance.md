# M7-004 --- Advertising Authorization and Brand Compliance

## Brand Consistency

-   All advertising shall preserve DELIKAT brand image.
-   Marketing communications shall remain consistent across franchise
    locations.

## Advertising Materials

The following material may require review before use: - Printed
advertisements - Posters - Flyers - Digital artwork - Local campaign
material

## Franchise Responsibilities

-   Submit advertising proposals before publication.
-   Include copies or samples of proposed materials.
-   Describe campaign objectives and execution.
-   Provide media, timing, costs and supporting materials.

## Franchisor Responsibilities

-   Review submitted materials.
-   Verify compliance with DELIKAT standards.
-   Approve or reject advertising materials.
-   Approve or reject advertising activities.
-   Record comments when required.

## Approval Record

Advertising authorization includes: - Franchise identification - Date -
Description of materials - Description of activities - Approval status -
Reviewer comments - Authorized signature - Authorization date

## Knowledge Note

Illustrations, sample advertisements, website layouts, Facebook posts,
Instagram posts and graphic assets are treated as visual assets and are
excluded from knowledge extraction.
