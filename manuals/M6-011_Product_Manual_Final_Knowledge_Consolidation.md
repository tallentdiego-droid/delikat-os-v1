# M6-011 --- Product Manual Final Knowledge Consolidation

## Product Governance

-   Official recipes define the approved method for producing menu
    items.
-   Only documented ingredients, quantities and procedures are
    authorized.

## Operational Sequence

-   Complete PREP recipes before dependent Kitchen recipes.
-   Store prepared components according to documented instructions.
-   Use prepared components exactly as specified by Kitchen recipes.

## Recipe Integrity

-   Maintain documented yields.
-   Follow documented preparation order.
-   Preserve standardized portion sizes.
-   Verify final presentation before serving.

## Knowledge Objects

The Product Manual provides operational knowledge for: - Menu catalog -
Standard recipes - Ingredient quantities - Production methods - Portion
standards - Packaging standards - Storage standards - Plating standards
