# M9-003_Business_Evaluation_Market_Analysis_and_Customer_Research

## Franchise Self-Evaluation

Evaluate: - Business structure. - Current products. - Additional
products that could be offered. - Authorized products. - Future product
opportunities.

## Competition Evaluation

Record: - Competitors. - Product quality comparison. - Product
offering. - Estimated market share.

## Customer Analysis

Identify: - Customer profile. - Residence. - Acquisition source. -
Customer expectations. - Reasons customers return. - Reasons customers
do not return.

## Customer Segmentation Categories

-   Gender.
-   Age.
-   Marital status.
-   Residence.
-   Distance from restaurant.
-   Visit frequency.

## Location Evaluation

Assess: - Commercial density. - Pedestrian access. - Public
transportation. - Vehicle access. - Parking. - Security. - Area
evolution. - Future area expectations.

## Activity Evaluation

Measure: - Sales growth. - Gross and net revenue. - Weekly customer
count. - Peak months. - Peak days. - Peak hours.

## Customer Survey

Capture: - First visit. - Ease of finding restaurant. - Location
convenience. - Service speed. - Waiting time. - Waiting explanation. -
Staff courtesy. - Staff appearance. - Most liked. - Least liked. -
Additional comments.
