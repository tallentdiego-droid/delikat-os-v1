# M4-004 --- Production, Receiving and Operational Processes

## Merchandise Receiving

-   Receive products against the corresponding documentation.
-   Verify product, quantity, quality and presentation.
-   Inspect packaging condition before acceptance.
-   Verify expiration dates where applicable.
-   Store accepted products immediately in the appropriate area.

## Requisitions

-   Kitchen requisitions shall be completed using the established form.
-   Record requesting area.
-   Record requested quantities.
-   Record delivered quantities.
-   Record responsible personnel.
-   Maintain requisition records for inventory control.

## Purchase Orders

-   Purchases for production areas shall follow the approved purchase
    order process.
-   Purchasing is coordinated through the designated supply function.
-   Maintain supporting documentation for all purchases.

## Kitchen Inventory

-   Record inventory using the established inventory format.
-   Compare physical inventory with recorded inventory.
-   Investigate discrepancies before closing the inventory period.

## Boutique Display

-   Merchandise shall be displayed according to established presentation
    standards.
-   Maintain products clean, organized and correctly identified.

## Price Setting

-   Apply authorized prices to products.
-   Ensure displayed prices match the approved selling price.

## Washing Process

-   Follow the documented washing procedure.
-   Maintain separation between clean and dirty items.
-   Return cleaned items to their designated storage locations.

## Cash Closing Process

-   Perform the end-of-day cash closing.
-   Count cash.
-   Verify transactions.
-   Complete required records.
-   Deliver funds according to established procedures.

## Personnel Selection

-   Follow the documented personnel selection process.
-   Maintain records of the hiring process.

## Pre-Processed Production

-   Produce pre-processed items following documented procedures.
-   Store products correctly after preparation.

## Food Preparation

-   Prepare menu items according to established procedures.
-   Follow approved recipes and preparation methods.

## Mise en Place

-   Complete mise en place before service.
-   Verify ingredients, utensils and equipment are ready for operation.

## Product Supply

-   Supply each operational area according to its requirements.
-   Record transfers where required.

## Processed Product Receiving

-   Verify processed products before acceptance.
-   Confirm quality and quantity before storage.
