# M3-008 --- Brand Manual Operational Standards

## Brand Manual Purpose

The Brand Manual establishes the rules for the correct use and graphical
application of the DELIKAT brand in every medium.

It is intended for all persons responsible for interpreting,
communicating and applying the brand.

## Brand Concept

-   Combine flavor and tradition with passion.
-   Maintain an atmosphere of nature and harmony.

## Corporate Identity

The DELIKAT identity consists of: - Symbol - Logotype - Corporate colors

These elements shall always be respected.

## Logo

The logotype is the primary identifier used across all applications.

## Isotype

The DELIKAT isotype is a heart whose upper half forms a leaf.

It represents: - Love - Passion - Fresh natural ingredients - Homemade
products - "Fabricamos Sabores"

## Protection Area

A minimum clear space equivalent to the "N" symbol shall surround the
logo.

## Corporate Typography

Corporate font family: - Futura Light - Futura Regular - Futura Bold

## Logo Versions

Permitted versions: - Full color - Black and white

Both versions shall preserve the approved proportions.

## Corporate Colors

Primary corporate color: - Pantone SW 6762 - Hex: #016D60

Secondary colors may only be used for: - Social media - Stationery -
Supporting graphic elements

Secondary colors shall never replace the institutional logo colors.

## Brand Applications

The logo shall always maintain: - Correct proportions - Approved
colors - Maximum visibility - Maximum legibility - Proper contrast

When used over photographs or non-corporate backgrounds, the white or
black version shall be selected according to background luminosity.

## Brand Assets

The following visual elements are treated as brand assets: -
Letterhead - Folder - Business card - Placemat - Wax paper - Stickers -
Coasters - Uniforms - Pizza box - Food box - Dessert box - Facebook
templates - Instagram Feed - Instagram Stories - Instagram Reels -
TikTok templates
