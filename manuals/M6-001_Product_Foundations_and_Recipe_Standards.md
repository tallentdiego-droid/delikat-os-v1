# M6-001 --- Product Manual Foundations and Recipe Standards

## Purpose

-   Define DELIKAT products, menus and standardized recipes.
-   Standardize ingredient quantities, preparation methods and
    presentation.
-   Ensure all franchises reproduce products consistently.

## Product Documentation

The franchisor provides: - Complete recipe documentation. - Recipes for
Prep, Kitchen and Bar. - Preparation videos. - Physical documentation. -
USB containing recipes and videos.

## Recipe Standard

Each recipe includes: - Recipe name. - Yield. - Ingredients. -
Quantities. - Units. - Preparation procedure. - Portioning
instructions. - Storage instructions where applicable.

## Mise en Place

-   Assemble all required ingredients before production.
-   Verify required utensils are available.
-   Prepare workstations before production.
-   Prepare recipes used as components of other menu items before
    service.
-   Kitchen staff begin sufficiently in advance to complete mise en
    place before breakfast service.
-   Kitchen schedules must account for this preparation time.

The manual contains the official DELIKAT menu reference.
