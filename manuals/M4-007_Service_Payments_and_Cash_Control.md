# M4-007 --- Service Standards, Payments and Cash Control

## Service Standards

-   Maintain restaurant presentation before, during and after service.
-   Verify menus are available and in good condition.
-   Replenish display merchandise before opening.
-   Conduct a final readiness review before admitting customers.

## Payment Reception

-   Accept payment according to approved payment methods.
-   Deliver the corresponding fiscal receipt.
-   Verify cardholder identity when required.
-   For large parties, determine billing distribution before taking the
    order whenever possible.
-   Follow local administration policies for split checks.

## Cash Control

-   Verify opening cash float.
-   Count cash during closing.
-   Reconcile cash with recorded transactions.
-   Verify authenticity of suspicious high-denomination banknotes using
    the approved security features applicable in the country.
-   Maintain all supporting documentation for cash reconciliation.

## Purchasing Administration

-   The Supply and Warehouse Manager centralizes purchasing requirements
    from:
    -   Kitchen
    -   Customer Service
    -   Patisserie
    -   Boutique
    -   Bar
    -   Prep
-   Review warehouse inventory before purchasing.
-   Purchase according to inventory balances and operational
    requirements.

## Operational Records

Maintain: - Cash records - Payment records - Purchasing records -
Receiving records - Inventory records - Cleaning records - Daily
operational checklists
