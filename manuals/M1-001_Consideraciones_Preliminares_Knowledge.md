## Confidential Operations Manual

-   Property of DELIKAT.
-   Must be returned upon expiration or termination of the franchise
    agreement.
-   Unauthorized disclosure, duplication or use is prohibited.
-   Violations are subject to franchise contract sanctions.

## Manual Organization

-   Compliance with the manual is mandatory.
-   Manual includes instructions, rules, standards, marketing,
    procedures and operational guidance.
-   Covered sections:
    -   Operating hours
    -   Personnel procedures
    -   HR procedures
    -   Job descriptions
    -   Franchisee obligations
    -   Store standards
    -   Security
    -   Customer service
    -   Marketing
    -   Brand usage
    -   Information systems
    -   Other operational procedures
-   Forms may be submitted by email.

## Manual Revisions

-   DELIKAT may modify the manual at any time.
-   Modifications are communicated through the Modification of Standards
    and Procedures notification.
-   Updated pages replace previous versions.
-   Partial changes replace only affected sections.
-   DELIKAT may perform scheduled verification, training and advisory
    visits.
-   Manual must remain locked when not in use.
-   Access is limited to authorized personnel.

## Confidentiality

-   Manual contents are confidential.
-   Confidentiality applies during and after the franchise.
-   Employees receive only necessary confidential information.
-   Updated manuals are provided during franchise validity.
-   Manual must be returned at contract termination.
-   Franchisee must keep the manual updated.

## Required Forms / Templates

-   Confidentiality Acceptance Letter.

-   Suggestion Form.

## Recurring Notifications

### Franchisor

-   New actions.
-   Pricing / merchandising changes.
-   Procedure changes.
-   Operational changes.
-   New products/services.
-   Uniform changes.
-   Marketing strategies.
-   Institutional activities.
-   Image/decor updates.

### Franchisee

-   Financial reports.
-   Key personnel changes.
-   Customer comments affecting brand image.

### Extraordinary

-   Local advertising approval.
-   Modification requests.
-   Installation approvals.
-   Legal/regulatory compliance notifications.
-   Judicial actions or customer claims.

## Modification Request Rules

-   New pages replace/add corresponding manual pages.
-   Franchisee signs acknowledgement.
-   Replaced pages returned to DELIKAT.

## Suggestion Rules

-   Suggestions submitted using official form.
-   Written justification required.
-   Financial impact and benefits may be included.
-   Suggestions intended to strengthen DELIKAT.

## Manual Limitations

-   Local rules must comply with manual and applicable laws.
-   Franchisee remains responsible for business and employees.
-   Legal and accounting advisors are recommended.
