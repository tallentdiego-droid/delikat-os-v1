# M7-001 --- Marketing and Advertising Governance

## Purpose

-   Advertising supports brand growth, customer acquisition and sales
    conversion.
-   Marketing activities must reinforce the DELIKAT brand and customer
    experience.

## Core Marketing Principles

-   Develop commercial and marketing activities.
-   Evaluate sales generated by promoted products.
-   Analyze customer preferences.
-   Innovate while maintaining DELIKAT quality and flavor standards.

## Four Marketing Elements

-   Product
-   Price
-   Place
-   Promotion

## Brand Standards

-   Maintain consistent service and product quality.
-   Protect the DELIKAT brand image.
-   Marketing begins with customer satisfaction and loyalty.

## Franchise Advertising Rules

-   Local advertising and marketing activities require prior approval
    from the franchisor.
-   Advertising must maintain message consistency across all franchises.
-   Standard promotional guidelines apply to every franchise.

## Approved Media Categories

-   Social media.
-   Traditional media.
-   Strategic partnerships.
-   Digital channels appropriate for the local market.

## Local Market Evaluation

Before selecting advertising media evaluate: - Target audience. - Local
relevance. - Costs. - Audience reach. - Market segment. - Expected
effectiveness.

## Promotional Planning

-   Prepare marketing calendars in advance.
-   Coordinate launches and seasonal campaigns.
-   Align local campaigns with brand strategy.
-   Measure campaign effectiveness.
-   Review results for future planning.

## Special Advertising Authorization

Special or local advertising requires: - Description of materials. -
Description of activities. - Timeframe. - Costs. - Materials used. -
Franchisor approval before execution.
