# M2-006 --- Visit Confirmation and Mystery Shopper

## Visit Confirmation Form

### Required Fields

-   Franchisee name
-   Address
-   Visit date
-   Visitor
-   Outstanding questions
-   Additional comments
-   Franchisee signature
-   Date

### Assistance Evaluation

-   Satisfied
-   Not satisfied with information received

### Franchise Progress Evaluation

-   Satisfied
-   Not satisfied

## Mystery Shopper Form

### Required Information

-   Franchisee name
-   Employee name
-   Mystery shopper name
-   Date
-   Time
-   Establishment location

### Evaluation Questions

-   Speed of service
-   Time until attended
-   Courtesy
-   First words used
-   Interest in customer needs
-   Menu explanation
-   Menu recommendations
-   Ability to answer questions
-   Robotic behavior
-   Focus on customer
-   Simultaneous customer handling
-   Uniform and presentation
-   Food temperature
-   Delivery time
-   Menu accuracy
-   Plate presentation
-   Allergy inquiry
-   Beverage or dessert upselling
-   Cleanliness
-   Utensil cleanliness
-   Employee relationships
-   Payment efficiency
-   Receipt delivery
-   Overall impression
-   Complaint handling
-   Unauthorized products offered
-   Customer discomfort
-   Service process explanation
-   Comments

## Mystery Shopper Rules

-   Questionnaire prepared by DELIKAT headquarters.
-   Questions may vary by season.
-   Questions may evaluate seasonal menu items.
-   Questions may respond to observations from social media or other
    channels.
-   Questionnaire is not disclosed in advance to franchisees or
    managers.
