## Responsibilities

### DELIKAT

-   Maintain and update the Confidential Operations Manual.
-   Notify approved operational changes.
-   Verify implementation through scheduled visits.
-   Provide training and advisory support.
-   Receive suggestions and official communications.

### Franchisee

-   Comply with the Confidential Operations Manual.
-   Maintain confidentiality during and after the franchise.
-   Keep the manual updated.
-   Store the manual securely.
-   Return the manual at contract termination.
-   Submit required operational notifications.
-   Notify significant legal matters.
-   Use official forms for suggestions and acknowledgements.
