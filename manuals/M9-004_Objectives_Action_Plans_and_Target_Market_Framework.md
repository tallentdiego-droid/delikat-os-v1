# M9-004_Objectives_Action_Plans_and_Target_Market_Framework

## Strategic Objectives

The manual identifies objectives in: - Sales growth. - Customer
growth. - Customer experience. - Operational efficiency. - Staff
development. - Social responsibility. - Customer loyalty. - Financial
optimization. - Product innovation. - Strategic alliances. - Product
quality. - Online reputation.

## Goal Setting Process

1.  Establish financial goals.
2.  Establish professional goals.
3.  Establish business goals.
4.  Consolidate objectives.
5.  Assign measurement date.
6.  Assign completion date.
7.  Review every six months.
8.  Adjust objectives when necessary.

## Business Goal Forms

Capture: - Financial objectives. - Professional objectives. - Business
objectives. - Ten-year projections. - Objective tracking.

## Action Plan Process

1.  Gather data.
2.  Establish objectives.
3.  Develop action plan.
4.  Implement plan.
5.  Evaluate results.

Action plan includes: - Target market identification. - Marketing plan
review. - Budget definition.

## Target Market Analysis

Develop: - Ideal customer profile. - Demographic profile. - Customer
comparison between actual and ideal. - Buyer persona. - Customer profile
questionnaire.

Customer profile includes: - Age. - Occupation. - Family composition. -
Residence. - Workplace. - Special characteristics. - Customer values. -
Attraction methods.

Visual templates (buyer persona, lean persona, worksheets and diagrams)
are treated as assets rather than operational knowledge.
