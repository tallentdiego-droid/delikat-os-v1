# M4-001 --- Daily Operations Manual (Operational Standard)

## Purpose

This module defines the daily operational standards required to
consistently operate a DELIKAT restaurant.

## Scope

Applies to all restaurant operations including: - Opening - Closing -
Kitchen - Service - Cashiering - Purchasing - Inventory - Cleaning -
Customer Service - Training

## Core Operating Principles

1.  Open prepared before the first guest arrives.
2.  Close leaving the restaurant ready for the following day.
3.  Maintain food safety at all times.
4.  Follow approved checklists.
5.  Record every operational activity.
6.  Protect company assets.
7.  Deliver consistent guest experience.
