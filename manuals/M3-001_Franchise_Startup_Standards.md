# M3-001 --- Franchise Startup Standards

## Glossary

### Franchise

A business model between two parties in which the franchisor grants the
franchisee the right to operate and commercialize its products or
services within a defined geographic area while transferring the
business know-how in exchange for periodic economic compensation.

### Franchisor

Owner of the original business and the party granting the rights to use
the brand and business model. Throughout this manual the term
**Franchiciante** is used.

### Franchisee

Beneficiary of the franchise agreement with the right to operate the
business in a defined territory under the agreed conditions. Throughout
this manual the term **Franquiciado** is used.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Introduction to Operating Standards

The franchisee shall not overlook commercial obligations and franchise
duties when opening a DELIKAT franchise.

Examples include: - Commercial identification of the premises. -
Insurance obligations. - Store location requirements.

Compliance with operating standards established by the franchisor is
intended to allow the franchisee to obtain greater business benefits.

This section contains the procedures and standards governing these
subjects.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Independence of the Franchise Agreement

Execution of the franchise agreement does not make the franchisee an
employee, representative or agent of the franchisor.

Although the franchise uses the DELIKAT name and logo, it must always be
clear that the franchise is an independent legal entity.

### Commercial Name Rules

The DELIKAT name may only be used when referring to: 1. Products or
services. 2. The franchisor. 3. Franchise business opportunities.

The franchisee shall not use DELIKAT as part of its legal corporate
name.

Acceptable: - Franchisee legal company name (example: ABC S.A.)

Not acceptable: - DELIKAT S.A. - Corporación DELIKAT - Cadena DELIKAT

All business documents issued by the franchisee shall display the
franchisee's legal entity.

Below the legal entity the following statement shall appear:

> "Esta franquicia DELIKAT es propiedad de ABC S.A. empresa que ha
> contratado con el Franquiciante la operación de una franquicia
> conforme a su sistema operativo."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Internal Sign

A public sign stating the independence between the franchise and
franchisor shall be displayed inside the premises.

The franchisor defines colors, dimensions and logo specifications.

Manufacturing costs are the responsibility of the franchisee.

The establishment shall also display franchisor contact information: -
Email - Telephone - Physical address
