# M6-006 --- Sample Recipe Operational Knowledge

## Recipe Relationships

-   Kitchen recipes reference PREP recipes when a prepared component is
    required.
-   PREP recipes produce standardized components for multiple finished
    dishes.

## Cooling Standards

-   Products are cooled following the documented procedure before
    packaging.
-   Cooling occurs before portioning when specified by the recipe.

## Packaging Standards

-   Use the container or bag specified by the recipe.
-   Vacuum sealing is used where indicated.
-   Every packaged product is labeled before warehouse delivery.

## Recipe Verification

-   Verify seasoning when instructed.
-   Verify cooking point before removing from heat.
-   Verify presentation against the reference image before dispatch.

## Ingredient Handling

-   Prepare ingredients exactly as described.
-   Follow the documented cutting method.
-   Follow documented cooking temperatures and times.
-   Do not omit recipe steps.

## Product Consistency

-   Finished products shall match the standardized recipe in:
    -   Ingredients
    -   Quantities
    -   Portion
    -   Preparation
    -   Presentation
