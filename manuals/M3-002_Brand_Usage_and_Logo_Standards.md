# M3-002 --- Brand Usage and Logo Standards

## Use of the DELIKAT Logo

Activities unrelated to operation of the franchise are independent from
the franchisor and this independence shall remain clear to customers.

The franchisor provides the official logo for use on: - Envelopes -
Signs - Forms - Letterheads - Business cards - Brochures - Promotional
materials

The franchisee's legal corporate name shall always accompany the logo.

## Brand Value

The DELIKAT name and trademark distinguish the franchise system from
competitors.

The logo represents: - High quality services. - Good customer service. -
Brand recognition.

## Mandatory Logo Rules

1.  Use the logo exactly as provided by the franchisor.
2.  Any additional use requires prior approval from the franchisor.
3.  Draft artwork shall be submitted for approval before production.
4.  Immediately discontinue any logo version declared inappropriate by
    the franchisor.
5.  DELIKAT trademarks shall never be combined with another corporation
    or legal entity.
6.  Immediately notify headquarters if another company uses similar
    names, words, symbols or logos.

## Logo Specifications

The DELIKAT brand represents: - Identity - Reputation - Corporate
values - Customer experience

The logo is the visual representation of the brand and shall always
match the approved version in: - Colors - Typography - Graphic
proportions

Interior and exterior image elements shall follow the Brand Manual
prepared by Etérea Collective.

The corporate image serves as the basis for: - Interior paint - Floors -
Equipment - Accessories - Lighting - Finishes
