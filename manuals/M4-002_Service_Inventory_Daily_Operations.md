# M4-002 --- Service, Inventory and Daily Operations

## Customer Service

-   Greet every customer upon arrival.
-   Deliver menus promptly.
-   Confirm split bills before taking large party orders.
-   Deliver fiscal receipt with every payment.
-   Follow defined payment procedures for cash, card, mixed payments and
    split bills.

## Purchasing

-   Consolidate departmental requisitions.
-   Plan purchases based on sales, inventory and cash availability.
-   Maintain supplier records.
-   Inspect received products before acceptance.
-   Monitor purchase prices.

## Receiving

-   Verify invoice against delivered products.
-   Inspect quantity, quality, temperature, expiration date and
    packaging.
-   Reject non-compliant products.
-   Record every receiving transaction.

## Warehouse

-   Apply FIFO (PEPS).
-   Label all products.
-   Do not store food directly on the floor.
-   Maintain cold chain.
-   Restrict warehouse access.
-   Record all inventory movements.

## Inventory

-   Perform scheduled physical counts.
-   Compare physical inventory against system inventory.
-   Investigate discrepancies.
-   Produce inventory, waste and purchasing reports.

## Cleaning

-   Follow daily cleaning schedules.
-   Clean dining room, kitchen, storage, bathrooms and service areas.
-   Verify completion before closing.

## Training

-   Complete role-specific training.
-   Participate in recurring operational training.
-   Complete operational evaluations.

## Complaint Handling

-   Listen to the customer.
-   Record the complaint.
-   Resolve according to company policy.
-   Escalate unresolved issues to management.
