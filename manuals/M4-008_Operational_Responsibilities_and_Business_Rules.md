# M4-008 --- Operational Responsibilities and Business Rules

## Opening Responsibilities

### Restaurant Manager

-   Verify the restaurant is ready before opening.
-   Conduct final operational inspection.
-   Provide final instructions to staff before service.

### Customer Service

-   Prepare dining room.
-   Verify menus, displays and guest areas.
-   Ensure customer areas are clean and organized.

### Kitchen

-   Verify equipment operation.
-   Prepare workstations.
-   Complete required mise en place.
-   Verify ingredient availability.

### Cashier

-   Verify POS operation.
-   Verify fixed cash.
-   Prepare payment equipment before opening.

## Closing Responsibilities

-   Complete assigned closing checklist.
-   Leave each work area ready for the following operating day.
-   Secure products, equipment and facilities before leaving.

## Inventory Rules

-   Inspect inventory regularly.
-   Record all inventory movements.
-   Support purchasing with inventory information.
-   Maintain warehouse organization.

## Documentation Rules

Operational documents include: - Checklists - Inventory logs -
Requisitions - Purchase orders - Receiving forms - Cleaning records -
Cash records

## General Business Rules

-   Follow documented operating procedures.
-   Maintain cleanliness and organization throughout operations.
-   Preserve food quality and safety.
-   Complete required operational records.
-   Supervisors verify compliance with assigned procedures.
