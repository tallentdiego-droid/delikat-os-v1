# M1-002 --- Forms and Templates

## Asset Registry

The following visual documents exist and are treated as assets linked to
operational knowledge: - Confidentiality Acceptance Letter -
Modification of Standards and Procedures Notice - Suggestion Form

## Confidentiality Acceptance Letter

### Purpose

Formal acknowledgement by the Franchisee to maintain confidentiality
during and after the franchise relationship.

### Commitments

-   Maintain confidentiality.
-   Do not own, maintain, consult for or participate in competing
    activities.
-   Acknowledge sanctions established in the Franchise Agreement.

### Required Fields

-   Franchisee representative name
-   Position
-   Company
-   Signature
-   Date

### Reference

The Franchise Agreement governs confidentiality obligations, authorities
and sanctions.

## Modification of Standards and Procedures Notice

### Purpose

Official document used when DELIKAT issues updated manual pages.

### Required Actions

1.  Replace or insert new pages.
2.  File them in the corresponding section.
3.  Sign acknowledgement.
4.  Return replaced pages to DELIKAT when applicable.

### Fields

-   Section
-   Page
-   Procedure
-   Date
-   Comments
-   Franchisee signature

## Suggestion Form

### Accepted Categories

-   Existing manual content
-   New procedure or service
-   Advertising, marketing or promotion

### Required Information

-   Description
-   Reason
-   Improvement provided
-   Franchisee signature

### Franchisor Use

-   Reception date
-   Reviewed by
-   Approved by
-   Rejected by
-   Decision date
