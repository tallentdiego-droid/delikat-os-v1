## DELIKAT History

-   Mediterranean restaurant created by Kathleen Schwartz and Gilles
    Tallent.
-   Opened in 2010 in San Salvador.
-   Initial operation began with five tables.
-   Philosophy: produce everything "from scratch".
-   Uses high-quality ingredients.
-   Focus on local ingredients prepared in diverse ways.
-   Restaurant recognized as a reference in El Salvador.
-   Serves more than five thousand customers monthly.
-   Customer experience is inspired by Mediterranean seaside
    restaurants.
-   Menu includes:
    -   Thin crust pizzas
    -   Salads
    -   Focaccias
    -   Shared appetizers
    -   French patisserie
    -   Red, white and rosé sangrias
    -   Specialty coffee
-   Core differentiator: CONSTANCY in quality and flavor.
-   DELIKAT supports franchisees to replicate the original experience.
-   Operational focus includes operations, HR, finance, production
    costs, inventory control and talent selection.

## Mission

"Servir nuestro menú preparado con los mejores ingredientes para
garantizar una excelente calidad con un sabor peculiar, en un ambiente
acogedor, limpio y una atención eficiente, enfocada en crear una
clientela frecuente y leal".

## Vision

"Ser un referente, para nuestros clientes en restaurantes inspirados en
la comida fresca del Mediterráneo, que nos permite replicar esta
experiencia única exitosamente en otras ubicaciones internacionales".
